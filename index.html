Mad Lib Generator

Remember all of those family trips where you and your siblings would constantly nag your parents asking them, "Are we there yet?", and they would toss you a paperback filled with, what your parents hoped, hours of blank Mad Libs? Well now you can use these three stories to make and infinate amount of reversions of the same three stories, which will entertain you for probably the same amount of time as the old paperback would!
